About Us

Welcome to the madness of our own theaters, dive into your favorite theater art. Whether you want to be entertained, scared, or see a family show, you've found the right place in the universe of this! We are a university theatre arts program (TAPS) that specialize in giving an audience an entertaining time in the university. We are dedicated university students with teachers in the theatre, and we have a variety of host shows to view! We hope to satisfy your needs and if you have any trouble, please feel free to contact us through our email or phone. We hope you have a fantastic time!
